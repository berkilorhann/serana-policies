# Privacy Policy — Serana

**Effective date:** July 5, 2026
**Developer:** CosmicLoopByte
**Contact:** cosmicloopbyte@gmail.com
**App:** Serana — AI Companion 

This Privacy Policy explains what information Serana ("the App", "we", "us")
collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the App you agree
to the practices described here.

## 1. Summary

- Serana does **not** require an account, email address, or phone number.
- Your identity in the App is an **anonymous, randomly generated device ID**
  created on first launch.
- We do **not** use third-party advertising SDKs and do not sell your data.
- Chat conversations are processed by our servers and an AI language model
  provider in order to generate replies.
- Payments are processed by **Google Play**; we never see your payment card
  details.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information you provide

| Data | When | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Display name | Onboarding / profile edit | Personalizing conversations and your profile |
| Age | Onboarding | Age-appropriate experience and verifying the 18+ requirement |
| Chat style preference | Onboarding / profile edit | Tailoring AI conversation tone |
| Profile bio and avatar selection | Profile edit (optional) | Displaying your profile in the App |
| Chat messages you send | While chatting | Generating AI companion replies and maintaining conversation history |
| In-app actions (likes/passes on characters, gifts sent, media unlocked) | While using the App | Operating matching, gifting, and unlock features |

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

| Data | When | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Anonymous device ID (random UUID generated by the App) | First launch | Identifying your device to our backend; this is your only identity in the App |
| Platform, app version, device language, and country | App start | Debugging, localization, and service operation |
| Purchase and entitlement status (subscription tier, credit balance) | Purchases and usage | Delivering paid features |

### 2.3 Information we do NOT collect

- Real name, email address, phone number (no account system exists)
- Precise location (GPS)
- Contacts, photos, files, microphone, or camera data
- Advertising identifiers for ad targeting

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

1. Provide and operate the App (conversations, characters, matching, gifts)
2. Generate AI responses to your messages
3. Process and honor purchases, subscriptions, and credit balances
4. Fix bugs, prevent abuse and fraud (e.g., entitlement tampering)
5. Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** use your data for third-party advertising and we do **not** sell
personal information.

## 4. AI-Generated Content and Chat Processing

- Messages you send are transmitted to our backend (Google Firebase Cloud
  Functions) and forwarded to an AI language model service to generate the
  companion's reply.
- Conversation history is stored on our servers, linked only to your anonymous
  device ID, so your chats persist across sessions.
- **Please do not share sensitive personal information in chats** (such as
  government ID numbers, financial details, health information, or precise
  location). Messages are processed as ordinary conversation data.
- AI responses are machine-generated fiction. They are not written by a human
  and do not constitute professional advice of any kind.

## 5. Payments and Subscriptions

- All purchases (subscriptions and credit packs) are processed by **Google Play
  Billing**. We never receive or store your payment card details.
- We use **RevenueCat** as a subscription-management service. RevenueCat
  receives purchase tokens and your anonymous App user ID to validate purchases
  and manage entitlements. See the
  [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).
- Refunds, cancellations, and billing disputes are handled through Google Play
  per its policies.

## 6. Service Providers (Data Processors)

We share data only with the processors required to run the App:

| Provider | Purpose | Data involved |
|---|---|---|
| Google Firebase / Google Cloud | Backend hosting and API (Cloud Functions), content delivery | Device ID, profile fields, chat messages, in-app actions |
| AI language model provider | Generating chat replies | Chat messages and conversation context |
| RevenueCat | Purchase validation and entitlement management | Anonymous user ID, purchase tokens, subscription status |
| Google Play | Payment processing, app distribution | Handled under Google's own policies |

We do not share your data with any other third parties except when required by
law.

## 7. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure; data in transit is protected
  with TLS encryption.
- On your device, the App stores only your anonymous device ID, preferences,
  and cached content (using Android DataStore).
- Access to production data is restricted to the developer for operation and
  debugging purposes.
- No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee
  absolute security, but we take commercially reasonable measures.

## 8. Data Retention and Deletion

- Your data is retained for as long as you use the App.
- Because Serana uses an anonymous device ID instead of accounts:
  - **Uninstalling the App** deletes the device ID from your device. Without
    that ID, the server-side data (profile, chats) can no longer be associated
    with you and becomes orphaned; it is deleted in periodic cleanups.
  - You may also request immediate deletion of all data linked to your device
    ID by emailing **cosmicloopbyte@gmail.com**. Include your device ID if you
    can find it, or contact us before uninstalling so we can identify the
    record. We will fulfil verified requests within 30 days.

## 9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live (e.g., GDPR in the EU/EEA, CCPA/CPRA in
California, KVKK in Türkiye), you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion
- Object to or restrict processing
- Receive a portable copy of your data
- Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact **cosmicloopbyte@gmail.com**. Note
that because we hold no account information, we may ask you to prove control of
the device ID in question.

## 10. Age Requirement

Serana is intended for adults and is rated accordingly. **You must be at least
18 years old** (or the age of majority in your jurisdiction) to use the App. We
do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18; if we learn that we have,
we will delete it. Parents or guardians who believe a minor has used the App
should contact us.

## 11. International Data Transfers

Our servers may be located outside your country (Google Cloud regions). Where
required, transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as the EU
Standard Contractual Clauses implemented by our processors.

## 12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be
announced in the App or on this page, and the "Effective date" above will be
updated. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes
acceptance.
